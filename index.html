Works automatically No tracking No data collection Runs locally

What it does

  • Detects excessive grayscale / desaturation effects applied across a webpage
  • Overrides those filters to restore normal colors
  • Helps improve clarity and readability when browsing

Privacy

The extension does not collect, store, or transmit any user data. All logic runs locally in your browser.

Permissions

The extension needs access to webpages you visit in order to detect and override color filters. It does not analyze page content for analytics or tracking.
